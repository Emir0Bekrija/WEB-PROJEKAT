Protein-Packed Chicken Crust Pizza

A protein pizza with a crust made of ground chicken breast, egg whites, and cheese. Topped with a low-carb tomato sauce, mozzarella, and your favorite pizza toppings.

Creator: Abby Madison

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground chicken breast
  • 2 egg whites
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup low-carb tomato sauce
  • Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the Chicken Crust:
    1. In a bowl, combine ground chicken breast, egg whites, shredded mozzarella cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
    2. Mix the ingredients thoroughly until well combined.
  3. Form the Pizza Crust:
    1. Transfer the chicken mixture onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.
    2. Use your hands to spread and flatten the mixture into a round shape, forming the pizza crust. Aim for about 1/4 inch thickness.
  4. Pre-Bake the Crust:
    1. Place the crust in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the crust becomes golden brown and cooked through.
    2. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  5. Add Toppings:
    1. Spread the low-carb tomato sauce evenly over the chicken crust.
    2. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
    3. Add your favorite pizza toppings, such as cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, mushrooms, or olives.
  6. Final Bake:
    1. Place the pizza back in the oven and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the toppings are cooked to your liking.
  7. Serve and Enjoy:
    1. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve. Enjoy your delicious protein-packed chicken crust pizza!
    2. This unique pizza offers a low-carb alternative with a flavorful twist. Customize the toppings to suit your taste preferences and indulge in a guilt-free pizza experience!
